About Crowd Tagging is an application that allows you to tag books without all the headaches that usually accompany the task. You can label key characteristics of any book in our database with ease. You have the option of voting on preexisting transcriptions or adding your very own.
HowTo You can signup or login above in the navigation bar. Once you signin, you will be redirected to our transcription page where you can start participating in the transcription process. You will notice a set of images on the left hand side and a set of options on the right hand side. These represent the different features of the book that you can tag. For example, title, author, publisher data, and so on. You can select a predefined tag or you can add your own. Once you select one for each feature, click the submit button at the bottom of the page to save your work. You must select at least one option for each feature to be eligible to save your work.
